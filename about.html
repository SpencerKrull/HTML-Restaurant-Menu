    Origin Story

    H-Thai-ML was founded by Swedish Chef in Booger Hole, West Virginia because he thought it would be funny. Since opening, we have served tens of people, as well as a few horses, allowing us to expand our Thai food empire to other towns with hilarious names. The New York Times once called us "barely a restaurant."
